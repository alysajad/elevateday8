6. My Research into VPN Encryption and Privacy Technologies

Understanding VPN Protocols Through My Research

During my project, I researched the various encryption technologies that VPN services typically employ:

OpenVPN Protocol:

  • Industry Standard: Widely considered the gold standard for VPN security
  • Encryption: Typically uses AES-256 encryption
  • Flexibility: Works across various platforms and network conditions
  • Open Source: Code is publicly auditable for security verification

IKEv2/IPSec Protocol:

  • Speed Focus: Optimized for fast connection establishment
  • Mobile Optimization: Excellent for mobile devices with frequent network changes
  • Security: Strong encryption with good key exchange mechanisms
  • Stability: Reliable reconnection capabilities

WireGuard Protocol:

  • Modern Approach: Newest VPN protocol with streamlined code
  • Performance: Significantly faster than traditional protocols
  • Security: Modern cryptographic principles
  • Simplicity: Easier to audit due to minimal codebase

L2TP/IPSec Protocol:

  • Legacy Option: Older but still widely supported
  • Compatibility: Works on virtually all devices
  • Security: Adequate encryption when properly implemented
  • Performance: Generally slower than modern alternatives
